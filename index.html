About SolGrid for installers evaluating the platform
Built & supported in Kenya

SolGrid is Kenya's M-Pesa-native Pay-As-You-Go platform for solar installers selling on lipa pole pole. A small controller goes on every system you deploy: when a customer pays via M-Pesa, their power unlocks in seconds — when they fall behind, it locks itself automatically. No exercise books, no door-to-door chasing, no dollar billing. You watch every unit, every shilling and every alert live, from one dashboard.

How it works from install to first automatic unlock in one day
STEP 01
Install the controller
Wire the SolGrid controller between the charge controller and the load on any 12V/24V system. Ten minutes with a screwdriver — no changes to panels or batteries.
STEP 02
Customer pays via M-Pesa
They pay your Paybill or tap the STK prompt from an SMS reminder. Payments land in your Safaricom account — SolGrid never touches your money.
STEP 03
Power unlocks itself
The payment is confirmed and the unit unlocks in seconds. Fall behind and it locks automatically. You watch every unit, shilling and alert from this dashboard.

Frequently asked questions
something else? ask us directly
How does the M-Pesa integration work?

You connect your own Paybill or Till number through the Safaricom Daraja API. When a customer pays, the confirmation webhook reaches SolGrid and the unit unlocks in seconds. SMS reminders can also carry an STK Push prompt so the customer pays with one tap.

Do I need to replace my existing hardware?

No. The SolGrid controller is a small unit wired between the charge controller and the load. It works with most 12V and 24V DC systems and common inverters — your panels, batteries and charge controllers stay as they are.

What happens when a unit loses network coverage?

The controller stores the paid-until date locally, so power stays on for whatever the customer has already paid even with no signal. When coverage returns it syncs automatically, and unlock tokens can be delivered by SMS as a fallback.

Are there hidden fees or per-payment commissions?

No. You pay the flat monthly plan price in Kenya Shillings and that's it — SolGrid takes no cut of your collections. Standard Safaricom M-Pesa transaction charges apply as they would on any Paybill.

Who owns my customer and payment data?

You do. Every customer record, payment and device log belongs to you and can be exported as CSV at any time. If you cancel, we hand over a full export and delete our copy on request, in line with the Kenya Data Protection Act.

Is there a long-term contract?

No contract — every plan is month-to-month and you can cancel anytime. New accounts get their first 30 days free, so you can run a pilot batch of units before paying anything.
