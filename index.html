About Lagriff for installers evaluating the platform
Built & supported in Kenya

Lagriff is Kenya's M-Pesa-native Pay-As-You-Go platform for solar installers selling on lipa pole pole. A small controller goes on every system you deploy: when a customer pays via M-Pesa, their power unlocks in seconds — when they fall behind, it locks itself automatically. No exercise books, no door-to-door chasing, no dollar billing. You watch every unit, every shilling and every alert live, from one dashboard.

Auto lock & unlock
Tied directly to M-Pesa STK Push & Paybill — confirmed and applied in seconds, no manual step.
Live fleet dashboard
Every device, balance and status — online, low battery, due or locked — in one view.
SMS reminders
Sent automatically before a payment falls due, so lockout is never a surprise.
AI fraud & maintenance alerts
Flags tamper events, wiring bypasses and fast battery drain before they cost you.
Carbon & MRV reporting
CO₂ avoided per unit, fleet-wide — ready for carbon credit reporting out of the box.
Priced in shillings
No dollar billing, no minimums — supported directly on WhatsApp by the team that built it.
